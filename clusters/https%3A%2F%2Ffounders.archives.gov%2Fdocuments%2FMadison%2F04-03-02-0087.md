#### From unknown source

July 4. 1823  
  
I herewith send you a copy of my late publication of the Official Letters of our Military &amp; Naval Officers during the late war with Great Britain &amp;c. in extra binding.  
  
The object of the compilation, you will perceive by the preface is, to hand down to posterity, the names and deeds of our gallant fellow citizens who so nobly sustained what is called our second war for independence.
