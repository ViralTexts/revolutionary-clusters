
# James Madison to John S. Skinner, 14 April 1829

### 19 reprints from 1829-04-14 to 1831-08-27

## James Madison to John S. Skinner, 14 April 1829

#### by Madison, James

#### From unknown source

#### 1829-04-14

<table style="width: 100%;"><tr><td style="width: 50%">

  
  
  
Montpr.  
  
Apl. 14. 1829  
  
  
  
I have recd. yours of the 24th. Ult; with a Set of the American Journal &amp;c. The value of such a  
publication, executed on the plan adopted &amp; with the ability to be presumed from the sample given, must recommend  
it strongly to the public favour, and I should very cheerfully offer my name for the subscription list: but that it is  
becoming every day more convenient for me to reduce rather than increase the attentions required for such objects.  
Under the i
</td></tr></table>
